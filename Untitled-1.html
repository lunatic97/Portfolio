My skills

My creative skills & experiences.

I have 27 years of technical experience in Research & Development, Technical services, Marketing and Technical training in the field of Metal Surface Treatment processes. I also act as a technical consultant in metal coating industries for plant set up, production, quality control etc. I have my fair share of experiences in formulating various surface treatment chemicals including multi-purpose degreasing chemical. Surface treatment chemicals include various electroplating salts and brighteners like acid chloride and alkaline zinc, nickel, copper, brass, chromium, silver, tin electrolplating, etc.... , Hexavalent and Trivalent chrome based passivation chemicals and also chrome free passivation chemicals, Aluminium surface treatment chemicals like various alochrome and anodising, electrocoloring salts, etc. ,Power coating pretreatment chemicals like derusting, degreasing, hot and cold zinc phosphating chemicals, manganese phosphating chemicals, heavy duty rust preventive zinc phosphate chemicals, etc., Hot dip galvanizing chemicals like pickling inhibitor, liquid fluxing chemicals, passivation chemicals.

Apart from these I have also worked on Refractory chemicals like Mono aluminium phosphate liquid, chrome oxide green sodium silicate, etc. and basic chemicals like tin salts, nickel salts, zinc salts, copper salts, silver salts, etc.
